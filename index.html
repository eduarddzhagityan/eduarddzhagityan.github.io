About

Eduard P. Dzhagityan is an economist, banking expert, university professor, and researcher whose work focuses on financial stability, macroprudential policy, risk governance, systemic risk, international finance, and banking M&A.

His professional career spans more than twenty-five years in banking and financial services and more than a decade in academia. Drawing on this practical background, he conducts research and teaches in finance, banking, financial regulation, and international finance.

Research Vision

Research in finance increasingly confronts the challenge of understanding how risk is created, amplified, and governed within modern financial systems. This research agenda explores these questions across several interconnected areas, including financial stability, macroprudential regulation, risk governance, and banking mergers and acquisitions.
