About

Professional Philosophy

Throughout my career, I have sought to integrate professional practice, academic research, and university teaching. Whether working in the banking sector, conducting research, or teaching students, my objective has remained consistent: to better understand how financial systems function, how risks emerge, and how institutions can become more resilient in an environment characterized by uncertainty and continual change.

Professional Experience

Before entering academia, I spent more than twenty-five years in banking and financial services. My professional experience includes international banking, cross-border operations, trade finance, international bank guarantees, foreign branches and subsidiaries, and strategic development of banking institutions.
